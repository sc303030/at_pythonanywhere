What Korean food do Koreans like?

What are Koreans' favorite Korean food? This is a visualization using kadx eating out consumption pattern data. Representatively, it can be seen that Doenjangjigae, Cheonggukjang, Stir-fried Jeyuk, Duruchigi, and Bulgogi ranked first and second from August 2020 to June 2021.
