Cooking JeyugBokkeum

  • 제육볶음

Thinly sliced pork marinated in spicy ginger-gochujang and stir-fried with onion, carrots, perilla leaves, or abbage. Ingredients vary according to taste.

Bokkeum refers to meat, seafood, or vegetables seasoned and quickly stir-fried over a high flame.

SERVES
serves 4
PREP TIME
15 minute
COOK TIME
15 minute
Ingredients
  • .Pork
  • .Various vegetables
  • .3 [meat undergarment (800g based)] Soy sauce
  • .2 chopped garlic
  • .0.2 black pepper

  • [making stir-fried marinade]
  • .3 Soy sauce
  • .7 Red pepper powder
  • .6 starch syrup
  • .2 chopped garlic
  • .2 grinded onions
  • .1 cooking wine
  • .little bit of black pepper and sesame oil
Directions
  • 01.Heat up a large, thick skillet (or pan) over medium high heat.
  • 02.Add pork, hot pepper paste, soy sauce, sugar, hot pepper flakes, black pepper, and toasted sesame oil.
  • 03.Mix it well, stirring with a wooden spoon. Cook it for a few minutes until the pork is well mixed with the seasonings.
  • 04.Add garlic, ginger, onion, and green onions. Stir and cook for 12 to 15 minutes until the pork is fully cooked and juicy and a little crispy.
  • 05.Transfer to a serving plate and serve with rice, kimchi, lettuce, ssamjang, and more side dishes.
